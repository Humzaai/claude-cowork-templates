# Client Communication System
**Save as:** `templates/18-client-communication.md`
**Purpose:** Consistent, professional communication templates for every client situation.

---

## MY CLIENT PRINCIPLES
1. Respond within [X hours] during business hours
2. Never leave a client wondering about status — proactively update
3. Deliver bad news fast, with a solution already in hand
4. Never say "I don't know" — say "I'll find out by [time]"
5. Document everything important in writing

## COMMUNICATION TEMPLATES

### Project Kickoff
```
Hi [Name],

Excited to get started. A few things to align on:

1. You will hear from me via [channel]. Best way to reach me: [channel].
2. I will send updates every [frequency].
3. First deliverable: [what] by [date].
4. What I need from you by [date]: [specific items].

Any questions before we begin?

[Sign-off]
```

### Scope Change Request
```
Hi [Name],

[New request] is outside the original scope of our agreement.

Happy to add this. Here is what it means:
- Additional time: [X hours or days]
- Additional cost: [amount or TBD]
- Impact on timeline: [none / adds X days]

Should I proceed on this basis?

[Sign-off]
```

### Delivering Work
```
Hi [Name],

[Deliverable name] is ready. Find it here: [link or attachment]

Key decisions made:
- [Decision 1 and why]
- [Decision 2 and why]

Feedback requested by: [date]

[Sign-off]
```
