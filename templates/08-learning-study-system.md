## AI PROMPTS FOR LEARNING

- "Give me 5 questions to test whether I actually understand [topic]."
- "Create a 7-day study plan for learning [topic] from beginner to working knowledge."
- "I understand [concept A] well. Explain [concept B] using [concept A] as an analogy."
- "What is the fastest path from zero knowledge of [topic] to being able to [specific goal]?"
