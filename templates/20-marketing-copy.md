# Marketing Copy Framework
**Save as:** `templates/20-marketing-copy.md`
**Purpose:** Proven copy structures for every marketing format — ads, landing pages, emails, product descriptions.

---

## THE FUNDAMENTALS
1. Lead with the customer's problem, not your product
2. Specificity beats generality every time
3. Social proof is more powerful than your claims
4. Remove friction from the next step — make it obvious and easy
5. Read your copy aloud — if it sounds like an ad, rewrite it

---

## HEADLINE FORMULAS
1. The Direct Promise: "[Result] in [Timeframe] — Without [Common Objection]"
2. The How-To: "How to [Achieve Result] Even If [Limiting Belief]"
3. The Question: "What if you could [desirable outcome] starting today?"
4. The Proof: "[Specific Number] [People] used [Product] to [Result]"
5. The Warning: "Stop [doing X]. It is costing you [Y]."

---

## LANDING PAGE STRUCTURE

**Headline:** [Main promise]
**Subheadline:** [Who it is for and what they get]
**Problem:** [Describe the pain they feel right now]
**Agitation:** [Show the cost of not solving this]
**Solution:** [Introduce your product as the answer]
**Features → Benefits:** For each feature, state the real benefit to the customer
**Proof:** Testimonials, case studies, numbers
**Offer:** What they get, the price, what makes it a no-brainer
**Guarantee:** Remove risk
**CTA:** One clear action

---

## SHORT AD COPY STRUCTURE

**Line 1:** The hook — stop the scroll
**Lines 2-3:** The problem or benefit
**Line 4:** The CTA

---

## PRODUCT DESCRIPTION FORMULA

Lead with the benefit, not the feature.

[Feature] → So that → [Benefit] → Which means → [Emotional outcome]

Example:
- Feature: Automatic scheduling
- So that: You never miss a post
- Which means: Your content goes out consistently without daily effort
