## POST FORMATS

### Short Post (Under 280 characters)
Structure: [Hook] + [Point] + [CTA or punchline]
Best for: Hot takes, reactions, quick tips

### Medium Post (280-500 characters)
Structure: [Hook] + [2-3 lines of value] + [CTA]
Best for: Advice, mini-lessons, opinions

### Thread (5-10 posts)
Structure:
- Post 1: Hook — make a bold claim
- Posts 2-8: Deliver on the promise (numbered points or story)
- Post 9: Summary
- Post 10: CTA (follow, subscribe, reply)

### LinkedIn Post
Structure:
- Line 1: Hook (the only line visible before "see more")
- Lines 2-5: Context or story
- Lines 6-12: The actual value
- Final lines: Reflection + question to drive comments
- CTA: Repost or subscribe

## MY HOOK FORMULAS
1. The Statistic: "[Number]% of [group] don't know [surprising fact]"
2. The Counterintuitive: "Stop [common advice]. Do this instead."
3. The Before/After: "[Bad state] → [Good state]. Here's how."
4. The Mistake: "I made a [costly] mistake. Here's what I learned."
5. The List Tease: "[Number] things I wish I knew before [doing X]:"
6. The Direct Address: "If you are [identity], you need to know this."
