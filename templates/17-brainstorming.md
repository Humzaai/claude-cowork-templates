# Brainstorming & Ideation System
**Save as:** `templates/17-brainstorming.md`
**Purpose:** A structured system for generating, filtering, and developing ideas.

---

## RULES FOR BRAINSTORMING SESSIONS
1. No judgment in the generation phase — generate first, evaluate later
2. Quantity before quality — aim for 20+ ideas before filtering
3. Build on ideas — every idea can spawn more
4. Invite the bad idea — the worst idea often leads to the best one
5. Time-box it — 20 minutes for generation, then filter

## CREATIVE UNBLOCKING TECHNIQUES

### The Constraint Game
Add an artificial constraint and solve within it:
- What if this had to be done in 24 hours?
- What if it cost nothing?
- What if we removed the most obvious feature?
- What if the audience was children?

### The Opposite Direction
State your goal. Now ask: what would make this fail completely? Reverse those failure points.

### The Forced Connection
Take a random object, brand, or person and force a connection to your problem. What would [Apple / a jazz musician / a chef] do with this problem?

## AI PROMPTS FOR BRAINSTORMING
- "Give me 20 ideas for [problem]. Include 5 conventional and 5 completely unconventional ones."
- "I am stuck on [challenge]. Ask me 10 questions to help me think about it differently."
- "What are 10 ways companies have solved a problem similar to [problem]?"
- "I have these 5 ideas: [list them]. Which has the most potential and why?"
