## WRITING MODES

### Mode 1: Educational
- Purpose: Teach something new
- Tone: Clear, patient, structured
- Format: Numbered steps, simple examples, no jargon
- Opening: Start with the problem, then the solution

### Mode 2: Opinion / Thought Leadership
- Purpose: Share a strong take
- Tone: Confident, direct, no hedging
- Format: Short punchy paragraphs, one idea per line
- Opening: Lead with the controversial or surprising claim

### Mode 3: Storytelling
- Purpose: Make an emotional connection
- Tone: Warm, personal, specific with details
- Format: Scene → tension → resolution
- Opening: Start in the middle of the action, not at the beginning

### Mode 4: Breaking News / Reaction
- Purpose: Comment on something happening now
- Tone: Urgent, fast, first-to-know energy
- Format: What happened → why it matters → what to do
- Opening: Lead with the news immediately
