## RESUME CONTENT TEMPLATE

### Professional Summary (3-4 sentences)
[Who you are] + [What you do best] + [What you bring to this specific role] + [One quantified achievement]

### Experience Entries
For each role:

**[Job Title] — [Company] — [Date range]**
- Achieved [result] by [what you did] — [X% improvement / $X saved / X users added]
- Built [thing] that [impact]
- Led [initiative] resulting in [outcome]

Rule: Every bullet must answer "so what?" — the impact, not just the task.

### Skills Section
- Technical: [list]
- Tools: [list]

## COVER LETTER TEMPLATE

```
[First line: a bold, specific opening — NOT "I am writing to apply for..."]

[Paragraph 1: Why this specific company, right now. Show research.]

[Paragraph 2: Your most relevant accomplishment and how it maps to their need.]
```
